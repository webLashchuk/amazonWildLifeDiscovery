FAQs: Your Antarctic Adventure Questions Answered

  • How do I book a tour with Amazon Wildlife Discovery?

    You can book a tour directly through our website by selecting your desired tour package and following the booking instructions. Alternatively, you can contact our customer service team for assistance with the booking process.

  • What should I pack for my Amazon tour?

    We recommend packing lightweight, breathable clothing, comfortable hiking shoes, insect repellent, sunscreen, a hat, a reusable water bottle, and any necessary medications. A detailed packing list will be provided upon booking.

  • Are accommodations included in the tour packages?

    Yes, all our tour packages include accommodations. Depending on the tour, you may stay in eco-lodges, riverboats, or jungle camps, all selected for their comfort and sustainability.

  • What is the best time of year to visit the Amazon?

    The Amazon can be visited year-round, but the dry season (June to November) is ideal for hiking and wildlife viewing, while the wet season (December to May) offers the best conditions for river cruises and canoeing.

  • How do you ensure the safety and health of your travelers?

    Your safety is our top priority. We provide experienced guides, well-maintained equipment, and follow strict health and safety protocols. We also offer comprehensive travel insurance options and have medical support available on tours.

  • Are your tours suitable for children and families?

    Yes, we offer several family-friendly tours with activities suitable for children. Our guides are experienced in working with families to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all ages.
