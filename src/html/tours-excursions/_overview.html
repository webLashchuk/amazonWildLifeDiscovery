Tour Packages Overview

Discover the diverse range of tour packages offered by Amazon Wildlife Discovery, each designed to provide a unique and immersive experience in the Amazon Rainforest

  • Jungle Expeditions

    Embark on thrilling jungle treks led by expert guides, where you'll explore dense rainforests, encounter exotic wildlife, and learn about the rich biodiversity of the Amazon. Highlights include guided hikes, night safaris, and visits to remote jungle areas

  • River Cruises

    Experience the Amazon from a different perspective with our river cruises. Sail along the majestic Amazon River and its tributaries, enjoying panoramic views and close-up encounters with riverine wildlife. Key experiences include onboard accommodations, stops at secluded spots, and opportunities for fishing and canoeing

  • Cultural Immersions

    Immerse yourself in the vibrant cultures of the Amazon's indigenous communities. These tours offer a deep dive into local traditions, crafts, and ways of life. Highlights include visits to traditional villages, participation in cultural ceremonies, and hands-on activities such as pottery and weaving
