Introduction to Amazonian Regions

Explore the diverse ecosystems and regions within the Amazon Rainforest, each offering unique characteristics and attractions. From lush tropical rainforests to winding rivers and vibrant indigenous communities, discover the wonders of the Amazon's varied landscapes

  • Upper Amazon

    Known for its rich biodiversity, the Upper Amazon is a haven for wildlife enthusiasts. Home to numerous species of birds, mammals, and plants, this region offers thrilling jungle treks and opportunities for close-up wildlife encounters

  • Central Amazon

    The heart of the Amazon Rainforest, the Central Amazon boasts vast, untouched forests and winding rivers. This region is perfect for river cruises, where you can explore remote tributaries and visit traditional indigenous villages

  • Lower Amazon

    Characterized by its sprawling floodplains and dense forests, the Lower Amazon provides unique opportunities for birdwatching and fishing. This region also features vibrant local markets and cultural festivals, offering a glimpse into the daily life of Amazonian communities

  • Amazon River Basin

    The lifeblood of the rainforest, the Amazon River Basin supports a diverse array of aquatic and terrestrial ecosystems. Experience the beauty of the river on a cruise or kayak adventure, and discover the incredible biodiversity that thrives in its waters
