Activities and Experiences

  • Wildlife Viewing

    Experience the Amazon's incredible biodiversity by spotting jaguars, capybaras, pink river dolphins, and more. Guided tours in Manu National Park, Yasuni National Park, and Tambopata National Reserve offer the best opportunities for wildlife encounters

  • Birdwatching

    Explore the Amazon's birdwatching paradise, home to thousands of species, including macaws, toucans, and harpy eagles. Top destinations for birdwatching include Tambopata National Reserve, Manu National Park, and the Central Amazon

  • Jungle Treks

    Embark on thrilling jungle treks to uncover hidden waterfalls, exotic plants, and diverse wildlife. The Upper Amazon, Manu National Park, and Lower Amazon are perfect for guided hikes through the dense rainforest

  • Cultural Encounters

    Immerse yourself in the traditions of Amazonian indigenous communities through cultural workshops, ceremonies, and village visits. Central Amazon, Yasuni National Park, and the Amazon River Basin are ideal for these enriching experiences

  • River Cruises

    Enjoy scenic river cruises along the Amazon River, offering panoramic views, wildlife encounters, and stops at remote locations. The Amazon River, Central Amazon, and Lower Amazon provide memorable cruise experiences

  • Night Safaris

    Discover the nocturnal side of the Amazon with guided night safaris, spotting elusive animals and experiencing the jungle's unique nighttime atmosphere. Tambopata National Reserve, Upper Amazon, and Manu National Park are prime spots for these adventures
