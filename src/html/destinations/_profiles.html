Destination Profiles

Explore a variety of enriching activities and experiences available in the Amazon rainforest. Each category provides detailed descriptions and recommendations for where to best enjoy them within specific destinations.

  • Wildlife Viewing

    Highlighted Attractions and Points of Interest: Explore the vibrant riverbanks, flooded forests, and numerous tributaries. Key attractions include the Meeting of Waters near Manaus and the lush Anavilhanas Archipelago.

    Popular Activities and Experiences: Enjoy river cruises, canoeing, fishing, and wildlife spotting. Visit local villages and participate in cultural exchanges with indigenous communities.

  • Birdwatching

    Highlighted Attractions and Points of Interest: Notable attractions include the Manu River, the Cocha Salvador oxbow lake, and the clay licks frequented by macaws.

    Popular Activities and Experiences: Engage in birdwatching, wildlife tours, and guided hikes. Experience traditional fishing methods and learn about local conservation efforts.

  • Yasuni National Park

    Highlighted Attractions and Points of Interest: Explore the Yasuni Biosphere Reserve, the Napo River, and the Yasuni clay licks, which attract various parrot species.

    Popular Activities and Experiences: Enjoy guided nature walks, canoe trips, and cultural visits to indigenous communities. Participate in night safaris to spot nocturnal wildlife.

  • Tambopata National Reserve

    Highlighted Attractions and Points of Interest: Visit the Tambopata River, Lake Sandoval, and the famous macaw clay lick at the Colorado Clay Lick.

    Popular Activities and Experiences: Activities include guided jungle treks, boat tours, birdwatching, and visits to the clay licks to observe macaws and other wildlife.
